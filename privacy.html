This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Facebook App for uploading videos to Facebook Pages.

Information We Collect

  • Page Access Tokens provided by Facebook to upload content on behalf of Pages you manage.
  • Page IDs to identify where to publish content.
  • We do not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

How We Use Your Information

The information is used solely to automate the uploading of videos to Facebook Pages via the Facebook Graph API. We do not use your data for any other purpose.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to ensure your data remains secure. All access tokens are stored locally on your machine and are not transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app interacts with Facebook's Graph API to automate posts. We do not share your data with any third-party services outside of Facebook's official API services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
